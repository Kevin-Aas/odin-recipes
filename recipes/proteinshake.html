Proteinshake

Description

To make a good proteinshake there you have to mix a good amount of proteinpowder with a good amount of milk. This drink will help you get the optimum amont of protein per day, namely 1 gram per pund of body weight.

Ingredients

  • Proteinpowder - two scoops
  • Milk - 300ml
  • Shaker

Steps

  1. Add a small amont of milk to the bottom of the shaker
  2. Pour all the proteinpowder into the shaker
  3. Add the remaning milk
  4. SHAKE SHAKE SHAKE!
  5. Serve in seperate glass or drink straight out of the shaker
